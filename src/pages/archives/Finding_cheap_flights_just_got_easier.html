| AIRLINES

Great News! Airfare Prices are Taking a Nosedive

Good news for wanderlust-filled souls seeking budget-friendly travel: a recent analysis by Going.com has unveiled a promising trend. Airfares have not only plummeted below pre-pandemic rates but have also witnessed a remarkable 24% drop from the soaring peaks of May 2022.


📉 Factors driving the decrease in airfare

One of the biggest factors decreasing airfare is the end of revenge travel following the pent-up demand that surged prices in a post-pandemic society. Katy Nastro from Going.com stated, “2023 marked a return to normalcy in flight prices, and as we step into 2024, we anticipate stability without major fluctuations except during peak travel seasons.” Economists agree, attributing the decrease in airfare to reduced leisure travel and a decline in business-related trips.


✈️ Business travel lags, impacting flight costs

Notably, Southwest Airlines revealed that business travel remains "below historical levels" during their October 2023 earnings call. This slump in corporate travel has become a significant contributing factor to the ongoing decrease in airfares.


Even though flight prices might be on the decline, it doesn't mean that you shouldn't be looking for more ways to save on travel. Keep reading below to see how to travel hack in 2024.

| TRAVEL PREDICTIONS

2024 Will be the Busiest Travel Year Yet —How to Hack it

The travel industry is poised for a monumental surge in 2024, predicted by Brian Kelly , CEO of The Points Guy. With nearly 2.9 million travelers passing through TSA screenings since Thanksgiving, Kelly's insights hint at a booming year ahead. So where will 2024 take you? And how much will it cost?


🗺️ Top destinations on the radar

In an interview with CBS, Kelly forecasted hotspots like Quebec, Cairo, San Diego, and Costa Rica as the front-runners for travel enthusiasts in 2024. However, he advised budget-conscious travelers to brace for fluctuating costs in popular destinations and peak travel seasons.


🏨 The rise of unique lodging

In a Bloomberg interview, Kelly underscored the trend of travelers favoring experiential lodging over conventional hotels. Immersive stays in tree houses, refurbished silos with breathtaking vistas, and underwater rooms offer unique travel experiences. Big-name hotel brands plan to keep up, meaning you can take advantage of hotel loyalty programs to score free nights while indulging your desire for experiential stays.


💸 Tips for savvy travel planning

The best thing you can do to still snatch up great flight deals is stay on top of flights. Consider setting up Google alerts to monitor price drops and jump on any deals.


You can also leverage tools like Google Flights' "Explorer" feature to scout pocket-friendly nonstop fares between different cities. Starting looking for international trips three to four months in advance and domestic travels six to eight weeks ahead to score the best deals.
